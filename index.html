How to Build Good Credit

Credit can be built many different ways this is a summation of serveral different options. If you are staring with no credit one of the best options is to start with a credit loan. Another option is to get a cosigned loan but make sure to let the cosigner know that the are on the hook if you default. If you already have started credit getting good credit can be accomplished by follow several rules. Make sure all payments are completed on time late payments can be sent to collections agensies and harm your credit terribly. Keep your credit utilization low getting overextended is a pitfall many fall into and can be hard to get out off. Don't open too many account at the same time this can hurt your average account age and will lower your credit score. Lastly keep account open as long as possible unless they have a annual payment.

  • Pay Credit Cards Off Monthly
  • Make sure all monthly bills are paid on time
  • Avoid opening too many accounts
